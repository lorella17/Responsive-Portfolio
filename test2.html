About Me

Born and raised in a small town where roads remain unpaved in Guatemala. My mothe migrated to the U.S when i was 5 leaving me behind with my grandma in search of the american dream. Saw my mother again at the age of 10 when she finally received residency status for her and myself. Moved to the U.S in 1995, lived in Los Angeles 2 and half years prior to moving to Atlanta. Attended The University of Georgia where i received my undergraduate degree in business management from Terry School of Business. Became the first member of my extended family to graduate from a University. Pay for my education working at a local logistics company, where i have reached my peak and looking for a new challege that will take my career to the next level.

During the last year i began considering a career change. I have applied to several different positions in the Logistics/Transportation industry, but have yet to find a new job. One of the main reasons has really been my loyalty to the current company i work for. Most recently after finally realizing that my career has reach a stagnant point i decided it was time to move on. I wanted a new challenge and be part of the ongoing changes technology and its fast paced field offer. This course will not be easy but if it were easy everyoen would be a software developer.
